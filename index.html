Referral Source: Agent Email Routing

This document explains who will receive the prospect action email based on the applicant’s selected referral source in the IPGS Admission Form.

Direct / Self Enquiry

Applicant came directly without agent, consultant, staff or referral.

Email Recipient Default admission / registry notification email.

Education Consultant

Applicant is linked to a registered education consultant or agent.

Email Recipient The selected active agent email from AGENT_MASTER.

Friend / Family

Applicant was referred by friend or family, but not through a registered agent code.

Email Recipient Default admission / registry notification email.

Social Media

Applicant came from social media channel or online campaign without agent code.

Email Recipient Default admission / registry notification email.

Innovative Staff

Applicant was referred internally by Innovative staff.

Email Recipient Assigned internal staff email for prospect creation.
Referral Source Selected Who Receives Prospect Action Email? Expected Action
Direct / Self Enquiry Default admission / registry email Create prospect in SKY or assign to PIC.
Education Consultant Selected active agent email from AGENT_MASTER Agent creates prospect in SKY and updates fee group form.
Friend / Family Default admission / registry email Registry/admission team follows up or assigns PIC.
Social Media Default admission / registry email Registry/admission team follows up or assigns PIC.
Innovative Staff Assigned internal staff email Internal staff creates prospect in SKY and updates fee group form.
Important: Education Consultant is the only referral source that requires selection of a registered Partner / Agent Code. Other referral sources will not use agent code routing unless the system rule is changed later.
